Tectonic

About the Game

Tectonic is an exciting puzzle game where you navigate through shifting landscapes and geological challenges. Build bridges, avoid falling rocks, and master the art of tectonic manipulation to reach your goal.

Game Features

  • Dynamic terrain that shifts and moves
  • Physics-based puzzle mechanics
  • Multiple challenging levels
  • Intuitive touch controls
  • Beautiful geological environments
  • Progressive difficulty curve
