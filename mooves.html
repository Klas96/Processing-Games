Mooves

About the App

Mooves is a training log app that helps you track your running sessions and earn QR code rewards when you reach your goals. Log your training, set distance and duration goals, and get rewarded with QR codes when you achieve them!

‚ú® Features

  • Training Log: Log your running sessions with distance, duration, and notes
  • Goal Setting: Set running goals for distance, duration, and time period (daily/weekly/monthly)
  • Health Connect Integration: Automatically sync running activities from Health Connect (Android)
  • QR Rewards: Generate QR codes when you reach your goals
  • Goal Tracking: Automatic goal checking based on your logged activities
  • Local Storage: All your training data stored locally on your device
  • Modern UI: Beautiful, intuitive interface with smooth animations

üéÆ How to Use

  1. Create an account or sign in
  2. Set your running goals in the Profile tab (distance, duration, period)
  3. Connect Health Connect to automatically sync activities (Android)
  4. Log training sessions manually or let Health Connect sync them
  5. When you reach your goal, get a QR code reward!
